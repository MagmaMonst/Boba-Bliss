What is Boba?

Boba, also known as bubble tea, is a delicious Taiwanese drink made with tea, milk, sugar, and chewy tapioca pearls or other toppings. It's a perfect blend of taste and texture!

Health Benefits

  • Rich in antioxidants (from tea).
  • Customizable sweetness for a healthier treat.
  • Add toppings like chia seeds for fiber.
  • Enjoy a refreshing and hydrating experience.

Flavors

Explore a world of flavors, from classic milk tea to exotic matcha, taro, and fruity blends like mango or strawberry!

Popular Toppings

  • Tapioca Pearls
  • Jelly Cubes
  • Popping Boba
  • Whipped Cream
  • Cheese Foam
